Ayat and Asar Uploader

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Ayat and Asar Uploader is a private internal tool used to publish and manage video content on the Ayat & Asar YouTube channel. It automates uploading videos, setting thumbnails, updating titles/descriptions, and posting source-citation comments on the channel's own uploads.

What This App Does

The app uses the YouTube Data API to manage a single YouTube channel that we own and operate. It does not access, collect, or share any data belonging to viewers, subscribers, or any other third party.

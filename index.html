Privacy Policy — Ayat and Asar Uploader

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Ayat and Asar Uploader ("the App") is a private tool used to publish video content to the Ayat & Asar YouTube channel. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

What the App Does

The App uses the YouTube Data API to upload videos, set thumbnails, update video metadata (titles, descriptions, tags), manage channel branding (banner, watermark), and post comments on behalf of the Ayat & Asar YouTube channel.

Data We Access

The App requests the following Google API scopes:

  • youtube.upload — to upload video files to the channel
  • youtube — to manage channel and video metadata, thumbnails, and branding
  • youtube.force-ssl — to post and manage comments on the channel's videos

The App does not access, store, or share any personal data belonging to viewers, subscribers, or third parties. It only interacts with content and settings belonging to the single YouTube channel it is authorized to manage.

Data Storage

OAuth credentials (client ID, client secret, and refresh token) are stored securely in a private environment and are never shared with third parties. No viewer or subscriber data is collected, stored, or processed by this App.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. The App's sole function is to publish content to the single authorized YouTube channel.
